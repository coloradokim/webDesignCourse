Planning for my final project

What is the purpose of the site?

Why is this site important? What is the most important elements of this site? What does a successful web site look like when this project is complete?

This site will be my professional portfolio. It will have an "about me" page that explains my background as an educator (degrees, experiences), and how I became an instructional designer. It will feature a portfolio of my development projects, and instructional design work.

Who is the target audience?

Knowing who this web page is intended for is important in helping you make the best design choices.

The target audience for my site is potential employers. I would also like kimschlesinger.com to be one of the top three results when a user searches for "Kim Schleinger" in Google.

What are the functional requirements?

What is important for the users to do on the website? Do you need a contact form? What about a blog or shopping cart?

Users should see a picture and desription of me, and be able to easily navigate to my project pages. I would like a contact form, and in the future, blog posts.

Who is in charge of acquiring all the content?

Even if this is just a site redesign, it is important to know who is respoonsible for all the content. This includes images, photography, video, and all written content.

Me!
